I knew fehu translated into “cattle” and meant “wealth”, and I just always assumed it was a rune to represent riches and prosperity (something I’d like to come close to achieving in my lifetime, but in today’s economy I don’t think even rune magic can help much with that). After reading the Rune Poems, though, I came to realize that Fehu also represents the negative aspects of wealth; the mistrust and treachery between friends and family when one suddenly becomes more wealthy than another.
